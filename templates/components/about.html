About Me

My journey into technology began after five years as a Critical Care Paramedic, where I learned to solve complex problems, make critical decisions under pressure, and lead teams in demanding environments. Today, I apply those same skills to software development, data analytics, and artificial intelligence.

I primarily work with Python, Django, SQL, Docker, Git, Flask, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and Scikit-learn, building projects that range from web applications to machine learning solutions. I am particularly interested in data-driven decision-making, AI systems, and creating software that delivers real-world value.

When I'm not coding, you'll usually find me exploring new technologies, studying machine learning concepts, exercising, or working on personal projects that challenge me to learn something new.
